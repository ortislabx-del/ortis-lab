# ORTIS Founding Constitution

## Preamble
We, the members of ORTIS, in order to establish a community dedicated to innovation, research, and development, do hereby ordain and establish this constitution for the governance of our collective.

## Article I: Name
The name of this organization shall be the ORTIS Organization.

## Article II: Purpose
The purpose of ORTIS shall be to foster collaboration among its members and promote advancements in technology and science.

## Article III: Membership
1. Membership shall be open to all individuals who express interest in participating in ORTIS activities.
2. Members are expected to uphold the principles and objectives of the organization.

## Article IV: Governance
1. ORTIS shall be governed by an elected board of directors.
2. The board shall consist of a President, Vice President, Secretary, and Treasurer.

## Article V: Meetings
1. Regular meetings of ORTIS shall be held at least once a month.
2. Special meetings may be called as needed.

## Article VI: Amendments
This constitution may be amended by a two-thirds vote of the membership present at any regular meeting.
